We rebuilt the Yonovo dashboard from the ground up. The new design focuses on the metrics that matter most to AR teams: total outstanding, overdue amounts, DSO trends, and collection activity.

Real-time metrics cards at the top show your current AR snapshot. Improved aging buckets give you a visual breakdown of receivables by age. Click any bucket to drill down into the specific invoices.

The activity feed on the right shows recent collection actions, payment receipts, and customer responses in a single timeline. No more switching between tabs.

The new dashboard loads 40% faster, with data streaming in progressively so you see key metrics first.
