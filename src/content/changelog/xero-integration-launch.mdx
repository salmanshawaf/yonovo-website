Xero users can now connect their accounts to Yonovo and start automating collections immediately. The integration pulls in all your invoices, contacts, and payment history so you can set up automated reminders in minutes.

How to connect:

1. Go to Settings and click Integrations
2. Select Xero and authorize access
3. Choose which organization to sync (if you have multiple)
4. Yonovo imports your data automatically

The initial sync typically takes 2 to 5 minutes depending on how many invoices you have. Two-way sync for Xero is in development and will launch in the coming weeks.
