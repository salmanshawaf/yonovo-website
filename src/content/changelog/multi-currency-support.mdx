Yonovo now supports invoices and collections in multiple currencies. Whether your customers pay in USD, CAD, EUR, GBP, or AUD, you can track everything in one place without switching between tools or doing manual conversions.

When you connect your accounting software, Yonovo automatically detects the currency on each invoice. The dashboard displays amounts in their original currency, and you can view consolidated totals in your base currency at any time. Exchange rates are updated daily.

If you already have multi-currency invoices in QuickBooks or Xero, Yonovo picks them up automatically on your next sync. No configuration needed.
